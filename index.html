RHA Exec Board

These are the positions on the RHA Executive Board you can apply for.

  • VP of Finance
  • VP of Marketing
  • VP of Advancement
  • VP of External
  • VP of Dooley's Bowl
  • Sustainability

Each Executive Board position has its own requirements and responsibilities, but there are some duties that apply to all positions.

General Responsibilities

  • Attend meetings on Mondays from 5:45 – 7:00 PM
  • Attend Hall Council meetings for respective Pods (more below)
  • Fulfill all tasks and responsibilities of the individual role
  • Display initiative and passion for the role and act as a student leader on campus, respresenting all of Housing

Pod Leader Responsibilities

  • Act as an advisor to Hall Councils by assisting them with both programming and advocacy work
  • Act as an information-relay along with the President to inform the hall on what the Executive Board is doing
  • Resolve conflicts and disputes that may occur between Hall Council members
  • Troubleshoot and resolve problems occurring within the Hall Council

VP of Finance

General overview of the VP of Finance role

  • Determine the initial allocation of funds across the organization in consultation with the President
  • Maximize the impact of every dollar in the organization by:
    • Finding and creating inexpensive but high-quality item packages for programming
    • Creating resources and guidebooks that incentivize the buying of inexpensive but high-quality items for programming
    • Identifying and resolving areas of waste across the organization
  • Create and implement a clear, open, and easy reimbursement procedure for members across the organization
  • Create and maintain an accurate log of all expenses and revenue for the entirety of the organization
  • Create procedures with Complex Directors for item deliveries to hall councils
  • Determine the best allocation of reserve funds across the organization
  • Increase the amount of self-generated funds we have by finding new vendors
  • Work to create an adequate budget for Clairmont programming
  • Work with External and Programming to approve funding for collaborative projects with other organizations
  • Coordinate and train Hall Council treasurers to ensure that they:
    • Keep a detailed record of all transactions on the Hall Council level
    • Maintain their power as the final check on all transactions within a Hall Council
    • Follow the guidelines created by the VP of Finance on buying items
  • Identify, document, and propose budgeting solutions for areas in the organization where an increase in funding is necessary
Apply for VP of Finance

VP of Marketing and Technology

General overview of the VP of Marketing role.

  • Create and maintain a brand for the organization by:
    • Designing new logos and updating those that already exist
    • Enforcing a standardized color palette for use across all media the organization uses to present itself
    • Determining a standardized typographic standard for use across all media the organization uses to present itself
    • Creating and overseeing the creation of flyers and banners for all events and initiatives in the organization
    • Designing and buying all RHA merchandise
  • Oversee all communications for the organization by:
    • Designing and maintaining the weekly newsletter, a communication distributed all of the 4,000+ residents on Emory's campuses
    • Reviewing and revising all emails sent to residents from all levels of the organization
    • Creating standardized guidelines and templates for how emails are sent across all levels of the organization
    • Maintaining and posting on all social media profiles (Snapchat, Facebook, Instagram)
    • Standardizing Hall Council social media accounts and distributing login information to the Councils before the start of the year
    • Designing and updating the Emory RHA website
  • Create a differential strategy for every level of marketing for all RHA events such that it reaches the maximum number of individuals at Emory
  • Coordinate all photography for the organization
  • Coordinate and train all Hall Council marketing chairs so that they:
    • Follow all standardized marketing guidelines
    • Know how to produce high-quality marketing materials to widely advertise their events
  • Oversee a Marketing Committee that assists in carrying out all assigned marketing tasks

It is recommended that applicants for the VP of Marketing position possess most of the following skills and proficiencies:

Software

  • Adobe Creative Cloud (Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, InDesign, Lightroom)

Experience

  • Front-end web development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JQuery
  • Photography, editing, and motion graphics
Apply for VP of Marketing

VP of Advancement

General overview of the VP of Advancement role.

  • Work with all Executive Board positions, Hall Councils, and Executive Committees to:
    • Identify weaknesses in the organization
    • Formulate and implement effective solutions to resolve organizational weaknesses and shortcomings
  • Oversee the creation of formalized documentation, procedures, and trainings which help optimize the organization
  • Conduct research and reach out to other Residence Hall Associations to identify how they function and to determine how to implement their best ideas
  • Present a bi-weekly report to the Executive Board on current initiatives being undertaken by the position
  • Draft and propose bills on the executive level that resolve weaknesses in the organization
  • Attend Hall Council and Committee meetings in addition to Executive meeting obligations
Apply for VP of Advancement

VP of External

General overview of the VP of External role.

  • Acts as the main point of contact for individuals, clubs, organizations, departments, and student governments who wish to work with RHA
  • Actively work to create and facilitate collaborations between all parts of RHA and other individuals, clubs, organizations, departments, and student governments
  • Create, manage, and update a directory of all outside contacts on Emory's campus
  • Assist the VP of Marketing in determining how RHA portrays itself to the broader Emory community
Apply for VP of External

VP of Dooley's Bowl

General overview of the VP of Dooley's Bowl role.

  • Control all aspects of the Dooley's Bowl competition:
    • Maintain and calculate the Dooley's Bowl standings
    • Decide whether events are part of Dooley's Bowl
    • Determine the Dooley's Bowl prizes
  • Actively work to determine the vision for the competition going into the future
  • Implement the mid-year and end-of-the-year prizes for halls
  • Work with External and the VP of Programming to reach out to other organizations on campus and create large-scale collaborative Dooley’s Bowl events
  • Work with the VP of Marketing to widely publicize Dooley’s Bowl on campus and increase its reach to Emory students
  • Something else about overseeing a committee
